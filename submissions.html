Call for Papers

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original unpublished manuscripts that demonstrate current research on resource-constraint machine learning targeting edge computing devices related to RCML workshop's topics of interest. Please use the US letter size (8.5 X 11 in) standard IEEE conference LaTeX format or Microsoft Word template available on this link.

All submissions should be written in English and submitted as a full paper. Full papers should have a maximum of six (6) printed pages including figures and references. Full papers are expected to describe fully developed ideas with a thorough evaluation. Note that RCML does not follow the double-blind review policy. The names and affiliations of all the authors must be present in the submitted manuscript.

Accepted and presented papers will be published in the HPSR 2023 conference proceedings and submitted to IEEE Xplore as well as other Abstracting and Indexing (A&I) databases.
